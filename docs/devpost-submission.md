## Submission lead

At an international summer school, I struggled to understand lectures and conversations in English. I also had little dedicated time to improve. At the same time, I was repeatedly waiting for AI tools to finish work. AIterval connects those two problems.

AIterval turns those waits into one short listening sprint. GPT-5.6 adds specificity: an upcoming lecture abstract becomes schema-validated practice that is played during the next normal AI wait. When the AI finishes, the listening stops and work resumes.
